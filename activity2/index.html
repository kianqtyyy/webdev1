Sporty Performance. Everyday Freedom.

The Yamaha Mio Aerox 155 combines sporty styling, powerful performance, and everyday practicality. Equipped with a 155cc liquid-cooled, fuel-injected engine featuring Variable Valve Actuation (VVA), it delivers smooth acceleration and excellent fuel efficiency for both city rides and weekend adventures.

What Makes the Mio Aerox 155 Stand Out

  • 155cc liquid-cooled Blue Core engine with VVA technology
  • Smart Motor Generator (SMG) for quiet engine starts
  • Aggressive sporty design with full LED lighting
  • Large under-seat storage for helmets and daily essentials
  • Digital LCD instrument panel with modern styling
